# Oat Bread

## Ingredients

| Ingredient         | Quantity |
| ------------------ | -------- |
| Yeast              | 2 Tea Spoons   |
| Flour              | 1 1/2 Cup    |
| Oats               | 1 Cup  |
| Water              | 1 1/2 Cup  |
| Oil                | 2 Table Spoon     |
| Salt               | 2 Tea Spoon    |
| Sugar              | 1 Tea Spoon       |


## Method

Soak oats in hot water for using 1 of the 1 1/2 cups of water included in the recipie
